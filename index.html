Philosophy

What “Size For Zero” means

Options can move +70% in an hour after being –90% intraday. That volatility is opportunity—and danger. “Size For Zero” means structuring risk so a trade going to zero doesn’t knock you off course. Expect zero as a possibility, plan for it, and let the winners compound.

Journal first

Capture setups, context, entries, exits, emotions. See reality over memory. Your edge lives in patterns, not stories.

Risk first

Size positions assuming a max loss is possible. If ruin is unlikely, time and discipline can do their work.

Data always

Tag and review by ticker, strategy, time-of-day, regime. Let the numbers tell you what to do more of—and what to avoid.

How does “Size For Zero” help growth?
